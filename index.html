🐺 Flagship — WOLF Language

WOLF Programming Language

WOLF is a purpose-built, AI-native programming language I designed and developed from the ground up. Unlike general-purpose languages retrofitted for AI, WOLF was architected with machine reasoning, declarative intelligence and autonomous execution as first-class citizens. It represents a fundamentally different way to write programs that think.

Designed from first principles — not a framework, not a library, but a complete language with its own syntax and runtime model.
Built to support autonomous decision logic, AI-native data structures and inference-driven execution flows.
The foundation layer powering WOLF Trading AI and future WOLF-ecosystem applications.
Reflects deep systems thinking: compiler design, language theory and applied AI architecture combined.
🐺 Flagship — WOLF Trading AI

WOLF Trading AI

WOLF Trading AI is an autonomous trading intelligence system built natively on the WOLF language. It goes beyond scripted algorithmic trading — it reasons about market conditions, adapts its execution logic and manages positions with an AI-first architecture. This is the practical proving ground for everything WOLF was built to do.

Autonomous market reasoning — the system interprets conditions and adapts without manual rule-coding.
Built on WOLF language primitives for native AI execution rather than external ML model calls.
Covers signal generation, risk evaluation, position management and adaptive exit logic in a unified intelligence layer.
Demonstrates real-world application of the WOLF language in one of the most demanding technical domains: live financial markets.
